How Staff Login Works

  1. Add staff member using the "Add Staff" button
  2. Share the generated join code with your staff member
  3. Staff member must use their email, password, and join code to login
  4. Staff can only access pages based on their role permissions
  5. You can remove staff anytime using the remove button
